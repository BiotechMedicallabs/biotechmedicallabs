Who We Are

Biotech Medical Labs is a mobile and in-office diagnostic lab based in Memphis, TN. We break down barriers to preventive and early detection testing by delivering fast, affordable, and accurate diagnostics to underserved communities — right where they are.

Why We Exist

In Memphis, too many neighborhoods are “testing deserts” — places where life-saving screenings are simply out of reach. I know the cost of that gap firsthand: my mother lost her battle with cholangiocarcinoma because, even though early testing was available, she didn’t always have access to it. Biotech Medical Labs was born from that loss, with one promise — to bring critical diagnostics to the doorstep of every person who needs them, so no one’s health is left to chance.
