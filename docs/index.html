Who We Are

Biotech Medical Labs is a Black woman–owned mobile and in-office diagnostic lab based in Memphis, TN. We break down barriers to preventive and early detection testing by delivering fast, affordable, and accurate diagnostics to underserved communities — right where they are.

Our Services

  • Mobile & in-office testing
  • STD panels & rapid HIV testing
  • DNA & paternity testing
  • Drug screening (employment/DOT)
  • Blood glucose & cholesterol
  • Flu, COVID-19 & strep testing
  • Pregnancy testing
  • Hemoglobin A1c & urinalysis
  • Send-out tests via Labcorp, Quest Diagnostics & DDC

Why We Exist

Too many Memphis residents live in “testing deserts,” where basic healthcare is out of reach. Inspired by my mother’s story — she passed from bile duct cancer due to lack of early testing — we exist so no one delays care because access is hard.

Partners for Advanced Testing

For specialized diagnostics, we partner with national reference labs: Labcorp, Quest Diagnostics, and DNA Diagnostics Center (DDC). This lets us offer rapid in-house results plus advanced send-out testing.
